About us

This application will have easy and quick transaction entry which will provide an option to the users whether to track the expense daily, weekly, monthly or yearly. Likewise, it will also set a fixed budget amount to help to meet users spending targets and carry over remaining budgets to the next week or month. Similarly, the users can also create a separate personal, business, and saving accounts and also can switch between accounts and view transactions for all accounts.This application will also have fraud detection capabilities which will identifies the duplicate identification. For those users who worry about security, the application will be password protected and also there is the way to deactivate access.
